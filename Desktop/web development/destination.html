DESTINATION

Spiti Valley is a beautiful cold desert located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Surrounded by the mighty Himalayas, it sits at a high altitude and is known for its breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and clear blue skies. The word “Spiti” means “The Middle Land,” as it lies between India and Tibet. The valley is home to friendly people who follow Tibetan culture and Buddhism. Visitors come to explore places like Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, and the village of Kaza. Because of its high altitude and remote location, Spiti has a very cold climate, with snow covering much of the region in winter. It is a perfect destination for those who love adventure, peace, and nature.

Kalpa is a beautiful village in the Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh. It is famous for its apple orchards and stunning views of the snow-covered Kinner Kailash mountains. The air is fresh and the people are friendly, living a peaceful life close to nature. Kalpa is also known for its wooden houses and calm atmosphere, making it a perfect place to relax.

Harsil, often called Hart Village, is located near Gangotri and sits beside the Bhagirathi River. It is covered with pine and deodar forests and is known for its peaceful environment. The cool weather, apple trees, and mountain views make Harsil a hidden gem for travelers seeking quiet and natural beauty.

Sissu is a small but charming village in the Lahaul Valley, near the Atal Tunnel. It is surrounded by tall mountains, green fields, and a famous waterfall that attracts many visitors. The village offers a mix of adventure and beauty, especially in summer when the valley turns lush and colorful.
