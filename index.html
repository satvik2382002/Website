Germany

United Kingdom

Famous Sights: Stonehenge, Tower of London, Edinburgh Castle.
What to Do: Visit castles, walk in parks, see West End shows.

India

Famous Sights: Taj Mahal, Gateway of India, Kerala Backwaters.
What to Do: Visit temples, try spicy food, take boat tours.
Fun Fact: India has rich festivals and delicious street food!
